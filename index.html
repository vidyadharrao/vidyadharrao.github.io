About Me

I am a PhD candidate at CVIT, IIIT Hyderabad, working under the supervision of Prof. C.V. Jawahar. My doctoral research focuses on the problem of information retrieval from scanned handwritten and printed document image collections.

I am currently a recipient of Alexa Graduate Fellowship (2018-2019). Previously I was a TCS research fellow.
